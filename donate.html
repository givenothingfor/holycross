Why Donate?

As a member of Holy Cross Parish, you've experienced firsthand the family feeling of our community. Even through the significant growth of the past years, the congregation has retained warmth, fellowship, and sincere connection among its members, and our members don't only worship together; they also engage themselves in daily activities and causes to benefit the community of Barangay Tejeros as a whole. This project is to gather and serve the least unfortunate to give them the needed nourishment, care and also compassion of those serving. Thank you.
