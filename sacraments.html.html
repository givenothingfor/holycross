Our Ministries

The Ministry senses that God wishes for him or her to devote their life to serving in the local church or ministry fulltime.

Ministry of Lectors and Commentators

They welcome the assembly before mass and also proclaims the word of God in the first and second readings.

   

Ministry of Greeters

The Ministry of Greeters is to provide hospitality and a friendly smile to attendees

   

Lay Ministers

Lay persons who assist the priest and deacon in distributing Holy Communion to the faithful during liturgy.

   

Music Ministry

Musical ministry is an important part of our parish life. Vocalists and instrumentalists assist the worshiping congregation in lifting its prayer to God.

Worship Ministry

The goal of the liturgy team at Holy Cross parish is to develop the worship and sacramental life of the parish so that all are nourished in faith and invited to Pray

   

Knights of the Altar

It is an organization that is formed by altar boys for preparatory steps to priesthood

   

Guardian of the Knights of the Altar

They are the support body of the organization, composed of the knights parents and guardians.

   

Parish Youth Ministry

Youth ministry focuses on inviting those who are not already part of the church into the deeper narrative of God's plan.
