Archdiocese of Manila

Holy Cross Parish

In the greatness of God and in the sanctity of the People of God, and the Church of Holy Cross Parish, for the believers of Barangay Carmona, Kasilawan, and Tejeros, City of Makati, Archdiocese of Manila, is solemnly ordained for holy worship of the Most Reverend Teodoro C. Bacani, Jr., D.D. this Fifth of September in the year of the Lord Two Thousand and Twelve, Seventh year of the era of Pope Benedict XVI. In this Holy Church, let us pray fervently to the Lord God so that we may remain strong in faith, undisturbed in hope, and steadfast in love.
